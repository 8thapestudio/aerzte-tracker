How it works

Four taps.
That's all.

Every interaction is shown live in the mockup on the right.

1

Swipe through your cards

Appointments are horizontal cards — swipe to browse. The nearest one is always first.

2

Add a new appointment

Tap the + card at the end of the deck. Name, date, time, phone — nothing else required.

3

Color tells you everything

Pink stripe = today. Cyan = soon. Amber = tomorrow. No reading required — just glance.

4

One tap to call

Touch the number on any card. Your phone dials immediately — zero navigation.

Features

Exactly enough.

🔁

Smart Auto-Sort

Appointments re-order by proximity. The next one is always first, always visible.

🏷

Color-coded urgency

Pink = today, cyan = soon, amber = tomorrow, grey = past. Zero reading required — just glance.

📞

Click-to-Call

One tap initiates a call directly. No copy-paste, no switching apps.

📦

Offline Storage

All data lives in LocalStorage. No account, no server, no privacy concern.

📱

PWA Ready

Install to home screen. Works like a native app — loads instantly, works offline.

⚡

Zero Friction

No login, no onboarding. Open and use — every single time.

Design & Stack

Principles first.

Design Principles

  • Minimal interaction per action — every tap matters
  • Visual clarity over feature density
  • Immediate contextual feedback (Heute / Morgen)
  • Stable under imperfect or incomplete input
  • No account, no friction, no data leaving the device
  • Mobile-first layout, touch-optimised targets
